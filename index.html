House Rules

By the book

Few in number, strictly enforced, occasionally ignored.

01

Finish the book

Even the one you've been side-eyeing since chapter three. No DNFs in this house — you can complain about it for the entire meeting, but you have to finish first.

02

Fresh reads only

Don't nominate something you (or anyone else in the club) has already read. The whole point is discovering together — recycled picks need not apply.

03

The wheel is fair

Books get picked by The Wheel, and the wheel believes in equal opportunity. No back-to-back wins — every member's nomination gets a turn before yours can come up again.

04

The override clause

Want to skip the wheel and just pick a book? You can — but only with a unanimous yes from every member. Otherwise, you spin like the rest of us.
